Snek

About Snek

 Source Code

Use the WASD keys to move the snek around. You are not allowed to bump into your own body or a wall, if you do, your game is over. Collect as much food as you can, which makes your snek grow in length. Your end goal is to get your snek as long as possible. This game was written in C with SDL for the graphics backend, and was ported to the browser in webassembly with emscripten.
